Who we are

A family operation that runs like one

Dickson Trucking is a family-owned heavy haul and dump trailer carrier based in Bath, New York, working the I-86 corridor and points beyond. We're small by design — which means when you call, you reach the people who actually move your load.

Every load leaves secured to DOT standard and tracked door to door. We'd rather turn down a job than rush one, and our repeat customers are the reason we've stayed busy for over 30 years.

  • Fully licensed, bonded, and insured
  • Late-model, well-maintained equipment
  • Direct line to dispatch — no call centers
  • Transparent, all-in pricing
